The Problem

A patient's blood sample is missing.
Nobody knows where it is.

This is not an edge case. In March 2025 at Nakasero Hospital — Uganda's leading private hospital — a CBC result took over 2 hours to reach the doctor. Standard experience.

  • No real-time visibility — staff cannot see where a sample is in the pipeline
  • No equipment intelligence — machines fail silently, results are delayed or lost
  • No QC automation — quality control done manually on paper or Excel
  • No early warnings — by the time a delay is noticed, the patient is already waiting
  • The alternative: WhatsApp groups and Excel files — the real competition
The Real Cost
Every missed sample is a clinical failure and a patient experience failure. At scale: patient loss, litigation risk, staff morale collapse.
The Market Reality
800+ lab-equipped facilities in Uganda. Most run on broken Excel files. Enterprise HMIS costs $50,000+/year — unaffordable for 95% of them.

The Solution

Kanta — The intelligence layer
above the record system.

We connect to any hospital lab system and surface real-time operational data — sample status, equipment health, QC results — on a mobile phone, with or without internet.

Lab Metrics Module
Real-time sample pipeline — where every sample is, from collection to result. TAT tracking and breach alerts. Turnaround leaderboards by section and technician.
Equipment Intelligence
Live equipment fleet monitoring. Error codes, runtime hours, reagent levels. Alert before the machine fails — not after the result is delayed.
Quality Control
Automated Westgard rules. Levey-Jennings visualisation. Reagent lot tracking. The QC workflow that costs $2,000/month elsewhere — included.
LIMS Data Bridge
Connects to any existing LIMS via read-only PostgreSQL bridge. No replacement. No disruption. No 18-month IT project. Observe and alert from day one.
